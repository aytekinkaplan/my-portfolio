About Me

My name is Aytekin Kaplan, and I am a passionate Software Development Engineer in Test with a robust background in both manual and automated testing. I have a degree in Physics from Istanbul University and a strong foundation in SDLC and STLC. I thrive in dynamic, fast-paced IT environments and enjoy working on challenging projects that push the boundaries of my knowledge and skills.

In my current role, I leverage tools such as Selenium WebDriver, Maven, Cucumber, JUnit, and TestNG to ensure software quality through rigorous testing methodologies. I am experienced in developing Automation Frameworks and have a keen interest in Continuous Integration and Delivery practices using Jenkins. I am also adept at API testing using Postman and have a solid understanding of SQL and database management.

Outside of work, I am an active education volunteer, conducting workshops and mentoring students to help them achieve their academic goals. I am committed to continuous learning and enjoy exploring new technologies and testing frameworks to stay ahead in the industry.
